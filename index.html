About Us

Education Should Not Stop Because of Money

Educare is a learning and donation platform helping students continue education through free mentor-led classes and community funding. We believe that financial limitations should never stop a student from learning, growing, and achieving their academic goals.

Our platform provides free learning tracks from Class 6 to post-graduation, supported by experienced mentors across subjects like Mathematics, Geography, Java, Python, and more. Every donation is used transparently to support tuition, books, stationery, and higher-study opportunities, including abroad education support for deserving students.
