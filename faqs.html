FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for students, mentors, and donors about how Educare works.

Is learning really free for students?

Yes. Students can access free mentor-led sessions and learning tracks without paying platform fees.

How can I apply as a teacher?

Go to the Teach page, submit your profile, and wait for admin verification before approval.

How are donations used?

Donations are directed to tuition support, books, stationery, and higher-study assistance for eligible students.

Can donors choose a donation purpose?

Yes. The donation form allows selecting categories such as higher education, abroad studies, and books.

Do students get certificates?

Merit-based recognition and completion certificates can be provided based on course performance.

Who can I contact for support?

Use the signup/login flow and teacher application channels; a dedicated contact page can be added next.
